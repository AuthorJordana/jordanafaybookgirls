About the Author

As an author, Jordana Faye finds her inspiration in the pages of countless books, fueled by a lifelong passion for reading. Her fascination with true crime often spills into her research, adding depth to her work. Off the page, she embraces life's finer details, enjoying fashion and a well-crafted cocktail alongside her literary adventures.
