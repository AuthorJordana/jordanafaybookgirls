Upcoming Books

Sovereign Storm

Expected: August 1, 2025

She was born to carry the storm—if it doesn’t tear her apart first.
Reigna Storm has always lived in the shadows—narrating other people’s stories while ignoring the strange visions that haunt her dreams. But when an ancestral power awakens in her blood, she discovers she’s not just a lucid dreamer... she’s the last Sovereign: a hybrid witch with seer blood and a royal fey lineage long thought extinct.
Hunted by Nythera, an ancient force of shadow who once shattered the magical Spiral and nearly destroyed the world, Reigna must form a sacred Circle—five magical bondmates who can anchor her rising power before it consumes her.
But each bond comes with its own scars. Trust is fragile. And love? Even more dangerous.
As their connection deepens and a forgotten war reignites, Reigna realizes that the greatest threat isn’t Nythera—it’s the truth of what she’s becoming.
And when a final, unknown bond ignites within her, one thing becomes terrifyingly clear:
The storm was never meant to be survived.

Crimson Pattern

Expected: October 13, 2025

Crimson Pattern is a dark psychological horror novel that delves into the intricacies of human psyche, trauma, and the twisted paths people take in the pursuit of vengeance and redemption. The story follows the protagonist, as they unravel the complex web of secrets surrounding their past, while being tormented by a mysterious force tied to an ancient and terrifying family curse.
The book's eerie atmosphere is enhanced by disturbing visions, a supernatural cult, and an unsettling connection to the past, all interwoven in a narrative that slowly reveals the horrifying truth. As the protagonist faces challenges and confronts their own inner demons, they are drawn into a series of disturbing events that push them to the edge of sanity.
Themes of guilt, survival, and the consequences of unresolved trauma play a central role in the story. The plot intensifies as the protagonist unravels the deep psychological patterns influencing their actions, leading to a shocking and haunting revelation that changes everything they thought they knew about themselves and their family.
The book explores the fragility of the human mind, the complexities of generational trauma, and the thin line between reality and madness, creating a chilling experience for readers. The suspense builds steadily, making the ultimate unraveling of the mystery even more terrifying.
